Visualizing COVID-19

An Interactive Dashboard Exploring the Global Impact of the Pandemic

Welcome to our interactive dashboard which is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of the global impact of the COVID-19 pandemic through a series of visually engaging and interactive charts.

Our goal is to help you explore the spread of COVID-19, its effects on various countries, and the impact of vaccination efforts across different regions.

Get started by reading the detailed descriptions of our visualizations and interactive features. Happy exploring!

Global COVID-19 Cases

To read and interact with the map, area, and line chart visualizations:

1. Identify the countries with high or low Covid-19 cases number based on color intensity
2. Observe the area chart to evaluate overall patterns and trends
3. Specify a time range by using the brush
4. Examine the line chart to determine the trends in deaths over time and compare it to trends in total cases

COVID-19 Vaccination Progress by Country and Continent

1. Read Instruction:
This bubble chart shows the progress of COVID-19 vaccinations across different continents. Each bubble represents a country, and the size of the bubble indicates the country's population. The color of the bubble represents the continent the country belongs to.

2. Interactive instruction:
2.1 Hover over a bubble to see more information about the country, including its total population, vaccinations, and vaccinations per person.
2.2 You can also hover over the legend on the right to highlight countries from a specific continent.
